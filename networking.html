Networking

Adirondack PC Sales & Service has trained professionals who can come to your business or home to fix your computer needs.

Our Tech's are CompTIA A+, Server +, Networking +, Security +, Microsoft Certified Professionals with various levels of training.


Wired Network

We will come to your business or home and work with you on a detailed plan to make your location networked. We have different options of equipment that we can use to fit your budget.

All installs are labeled and include a layout diagram.

Wireless Networking

Whether you need to add 1 wireless access point or 100 we can work with you to give you the best coverage for your location.

Our wireless networking equipment lets you assign secured keys to make it secured or leave it open for anyone to connect to.

The equipment we use can be setup for metering, this is perfect for resorts if you want to limit the amount of bandwidth your guests are using.
